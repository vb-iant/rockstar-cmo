***Thanks and a roundup of another splendid month on the podcast, with some fascinating guests, insightful cocktails, and some fun by the Rockstar CMO Swimming pool.***

---

![](/images/blog/a97142189641.png)

As it's the Thanksgiving issue, I definitely want to be thankful for this podcast, it's a labor of love for sure, way more work than I realized, but it's fun.

I'm thankful for the audience we are building; as I ask every week, does the world need another f'in marketing podcast? Well, at last count, we've been listened to in 46 countries, not bad for such a new kid on the podcasting block.

But, most of all, I'm thankful for the conversations it has given me the excuse to have with our Rockstar CMO community, people that have influenced my career, and some brand new folks that I might never have met otherwise.

Special thanks to Robert Rose, who shares a cocktail and one marketing thought in our virtual Rockstar CMO bar every week.

This month continues the thread of an eclectic band of marketing thinkers, either the lead singers or those that play the instruments that make up a marketing band. I hope you'll give them a listen.

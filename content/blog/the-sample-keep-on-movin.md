The first article I'd like you to take a look at is by Robert Rose, a resident here at Rockstar CMO, but also The Chief Trouble Maker at [The Content Advisory](https://contentadvisory.net/) and in his wonderful article [Are We There Yet?](https://contentadvisory.net/are-we-there-yet/) he talks about patience, something we probably all need a little of as life gets uncertain. ## Thoughts on Relationships, Work, and Challenging Times

![Thoughts on Relationships, Work, and Challenging Times](https://i2.wp.com/tedrubin.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/NoLetUp.jpg?resize=1080%2C675&ssl=1)

Speaking of resident Rockstar CMO contributors, over on [his own blog](https://tedrubin.com/thoughts-on-relationships-work-and-challenging-times/) Ted Rubin has written a very extensive piece on the importance of relationships right now that pulls together a lot of what Ted talks about, with his "return on relationships" ethos. [[ Read more ]](https://tedrubin.com/thoughts-on-relationships-work-and-challenging-times/)

> One thing that’s relevant to a lot of what we’ve talked about here (especially in this environment) is: **A brand is what a business, or a person does; a [reputation](https://tedrubin.com/brands-say-what-you-mean-mean-what-you-say/) is what people remember and share.** Brands are so busy trying to sell people things at a time when their customers have other things to think about. It’s insulting to the customer. Please take this to heart.

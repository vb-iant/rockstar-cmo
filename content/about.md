# About Rockstar CMO

Does the world need another f'in' marketing podcast? Find out, with Ian Truscott, as he uses this podcast as an excuse to chat with friends old and new he's met through his marketing career, from techie to CMO, sharing the marketing street knowledge that will hopefully bring out the Rockstar CMO in you.

Host Ian Truscott is a techie turned CMO. While he says he's no rockstar, in this weekly podcast he chats to the true rockstars, his chums Jeff Clark and Robert Rose, plus special guests, who share a wide range of practical experience and advice they call marketing street knowledge.
